RESULTS OF THE 2002 EuCW/FISTS QRS PARTY

Once again an interesting set of logs has been received although not as many as I had hoped for. What was apparent from the logs was that most people enjoyed the event again and it was nice to see some new callsigns.

This year our eldest entrant went one better than last year and won the event, many congratulations Dick, G8NT. The score Dick achieved was nearly 11,000 higher than last year's winner and more than double his last years score. It goes to prove that with some effort QSO's can be found and good scores assembled.

Congratulations to all the certificate winners and especially G0EML, Ray, for winning the "Most Readable Morse Heard" during the event. It was interesting to note that, like last year's winner of this title, this was Ray's first contest.
