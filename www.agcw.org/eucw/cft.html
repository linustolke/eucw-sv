CFT Club Francophone Télégraphiste

This Belgian club is dedicated to CW in ham radio and to ham spirit in general. The club originates from the French speaking areas of Belgium but international members are welcome, too.

To become a member you have to contact the founders of the club: ON4KML - ON4LDL - ON4LEX - ON4RU - ON4KMB - ON4WC - ON4KRS or their club station ON4CPN, preferably on Friday evenings on 3520 Khz at 21h local time. QSO duration must be 15 min or more.

Preferred QRGs 3520, 7020, 14020, 21020 kHz.
