HCC - Hispania CW Club

Since 1983 this club is active in Spain promoting the art of CW. Presently it counts about 500 members. Membership is open to any holder of a Spanish license.

Awards

  • SPA: Pintores Españoles ("Guernika" y "la Maja Desnuda") also called SPA (Spanish Painters Award)
  • 2CSM: Segundo Centenario Samuel Morse (Second Century of Samuel Morse)
  • DAEACW: Diploma Autonomías EACW

Contests:

  • Encuentro con el vertical
  • RAMI
  • MARATON
  • CRONOS
