Results 2000

Date: Swedish MID-SUMMER DAY UTC: 0600-1800

Frequencies:
80 m: 3540 kHz - 3570 kHz
40 m: 7020 kHz - 7040 kHz
30 m: entire band
20 m: 14050 kHz - 14060 kHz

Exchange: Normal QSOs using straight keys, the SKD is NOT a contest. Each participant with 5 or more QSOs may give three 'votes' to stations with a 'good fist', i.e. a good Morse code transmitting style.

Awards: Stations that get two or more such 'votes' will receive an SKD award
