LZCWC: LZ CW Club - Contest group

    Addr.: Antoaneta Encheva, LZ2AU P.O.Box 189 Gorna Oryahovitza, 5100 BULGARIA

    Club activites: Round Tables every Tuesday at 18.00Z on 3527 kHz, net control LZ9CWC, licensed to and operated by LZ1AF for LZCWC purposes. During the Round Tables nominations to membership are made and voted. A mini contest every last Thursday of the month 18.00 - 19.00Z 3530-3570 kHz. The LZCWC issues to members a Certificate for Excellent CW Operating. Nominations and voting at Round Tables.
