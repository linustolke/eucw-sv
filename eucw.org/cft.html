CFT Club Francophone Télégraphiste

This Belgian club is dedicated to CW in ham radio and to ham spirit in general. The club originates from the French speaking areas of Belgium but international members are welcome, too.

Preferred QRGs 3520, 7020, 14020, 21020 kHz. Check the CFT home page for the regular skeds or ask via the club email on5cft (at_symbol) gmail . com .
