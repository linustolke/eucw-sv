The Italian key producer Pietro Begali, I2RTF, donates two keys to lucky participants of the EUCW Snakes and Ladders Activity

The first key will be chosen from the pool of those participants who have submitted more than NINE monthly logs to the EUCW Snakes and Ladders Activity.

The second key will be donated as a gift to the particpant which happens to have the highest number of points after the 12th month of the EUCW Snakes and Ladders Activity.

Thanks a lot for the generous offer and support to EUCW.
