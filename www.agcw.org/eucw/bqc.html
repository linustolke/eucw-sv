BQC: Benelux QRP Club
    This club is dedicated to QRP which is defined as 5W HF output. The club is open to all QRP enthusiasts and publishes a quarterly magazine written in Dutch. The magazine addresses QRP operators and home brewers. Contact address is: Benelux QRP Club, Postbus 15, 2100 AA, Heemstede, The Netherlands.
