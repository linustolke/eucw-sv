HSC: High Speed Club
is for the friends of moderately high CW speeds, i.e. 25 wpm and above. There is no annual fee. The club organizes contests and awards. The one and only way to become a member is via hi-speed sponsorship QSOs with 5 different HSC members. These sponsorship QSOs must be confirmed by QSLs specifying the CW speed and and the QSO duration.
